The AI Research Atlas

By Lee Fischman

A live semantic atlas of recent AI research from arXiv (cs.AI), rebuilt daily. Each point is a paper. Nearby points share similar topics — clusters surface naturally from the embedding space and are labelled by their most distinctive terms.

Powered by Apple Embedding Atlas and SPECTER2 scientific embeddings.
