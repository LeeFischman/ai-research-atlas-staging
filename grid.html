The AI Research Atlas

By Lee Fischman

A live semantic map of recent AI research from arXiv (cs.AI), rebuilt every day across a rolling 14-day window. Each point is a paper. Groups are determined by Claude Haiku based on shared research methodology and problem formulation.

How to use

Sort columns by clicking their headers. Use the Shortcuts panel to filter by author prominence, citation impact, or recency. Click a row’s abstract cell to expand it. Click a paper title to open it on arXiv.
